HOW TO PREPARE GARI SOAKINGS

INGREDIENTS

  • Gari
  • Sugar
  • Roasted groundnut
  • Water
  • Milk

TOOLS

  • Spoon
  • Bowl

METHOD

  1. Pour amount of gari into a bowl.
  2. Add sugar to taste.
  3. Add roasted groundnut, milk and water to make it light.
    • when adding, water must be the last ingredient;
    • first, add milk
    • then add roasted groundnut
    • lastly, add water
  4. Stir thoroughly to desolve the sugar with spoon
  5. Food is READY.
